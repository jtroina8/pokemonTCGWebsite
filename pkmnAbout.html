How This Works

  • Type a Pokemon in the search bar
  • Press Enter to Search
  • See results!

    • The Results

  • The results will appear with the images of every Pokemon card made for that Pokemon
  • These images are clickable!
  • By clicking whatever card you choose, it will take you to the TCGPlayer link for that card.
  • This will tell you how much your card is worth, and how much you can currently buy it for.
